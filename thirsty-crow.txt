**The Thirsty Crow**

Once upon a time, there was a thirsty crow flying in search of water. After a long time, he found a pitcher with a little water at the bottom.

The crow tried to drink the water, but his beak could not reach it. He looked around and found some small stones.

One by one, the crow dropped the stones into the pitcher. Slowly, the water level rose until it reached the top.

The crow drank the water and flew away happily.

**Moral:** Where there is a will, there is a way.
