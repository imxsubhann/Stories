Once upon a time, a hungry dog found a big bone. He picked it up and happily carried it home.

On the way, he had to cross a bridge over a river. As he looked into the water, he saw his own reflection. Thinking it was another dog with a bigger bone, he became greedy.

The dog barked to grab the other bone. As soon as he opened his mouth, his own bone fell into the river and was washed away.

The greedy dog lost the bone because of his greed.
