Once upon a time, a mighty lion was sleeping peacefully in the jungle. A little rat accidentally ran across the lion's paw and woke him up.

The angry lion caught the tiny rat and said, "How dare you disturb my sleep? I should eat you!"

The frightened rat pleaded, "Please let me go. One day I may be able to help you."

The lion laughed because the rat was so small, but he decided to be kind and let the rat go.

A few days later, the lion was trapped in a hunter's net. He roared loudly for help. The little rat heard the roar and quickly ran to the lion. Using its sharp teeth, the rat chewed through the ropes until the lion was free.

The lion smiled and said, "Thank you, little friend. I never believed someone so small could help someone so big."

Moral: Kindness is never wasted, and even the smallest friend can make a big difference.
